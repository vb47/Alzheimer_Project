The AI Doctor

About us

The AI doctor is an Artificial Intelligence based project developed for deep learning based analysis on the Surveys taken. This project is developed by Abhishek Anvekar, Abhishek Kolvekar, Vaibhav Alur and Yash Joshi. The project features on the go results on the surveys taken. It also displays graphical visualisations developed by our team expert to provide statistical analysis.
